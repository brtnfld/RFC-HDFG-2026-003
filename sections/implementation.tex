\section{Implementation Notes}
\label{sec:impl}

This section maps the design in the preceding sections to the library
source files it touches.

\begin{center}
\small
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.25}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{lX}
\toprule
\textbf{File} & \textbf{Change} \\
\midrule
\texttt{src/H5Zdevelop.h} & Add \texttt{H5Z\_blob\_loc\_t} and the three
  blob callback typedefs; add the three fields to \texttt{H5Z\_class3\_t}
  (\SectionRef{sec:blob-callbacks}). \\
\texttt{src/H5Zprivate.h} & Add \texttt{aux\_data}, \texttt{aux\_size},
  \texttt{aux\_loc} to \texttt{H5Z\_filter\_info\_t}
  (\SectionRef{sec:blob-internal-state}). \\
\texttt{src/H5Z.c} & Wire up \texttt{write\_blob}/\texttt{read\_blob}/
  \texttt{close\_blob} (or the default \texttt{H5HG}-based
  implementation) into the create/open/close paths
  (\SectionRef{sec:blob-lifecycle}); implement the rank-0-write-and-broadcast
  protocol for parallel jobs (\SectionRef{sec:blob-parallel}). \\
\texttt{src/H5Pocpl.c} & Implement \texttt{H5Pappend\_filter\_blob}
  (\SectionRef{sec:append-filter-blob}); extend
  \texttt{H5P\_\_ocrt\_pipeline\_enc}/\texttt{\_dec} to serialize
  \texttt{aux\_data} inline (\SectionRef{sec:blob-encode-decode}); extend
  the pipeline-copy callback to deep-copy \texttt{aux\_data}
  (\SectionRef{sec:blob-repack}). \\
\texttt{src/H5Opline.c} & Pipeline-message encode/decode of
  \texttt{has\_aux}/\texttt{aux\_loc} (\SectionRef{sec:blob-format}); the
  \texttt{delete} handler calls \texttt{H5HG\_remove} to reclaim heap
  space when a blob-bearing filter entry or dataset is deleted. \\
\texttt{src/H5Dchunk.c} & No changes. The runtime chunk I/O path
  (\texttt{H5Z\_pipeline}) is unaffected; the blob is configuration data
  loaded once at open time. \\
\texttt{tools/src/h5repack/} & No changes needed; see
  \SectionRef{sec:blob-repack}. \\
\texttt{test/} & New tests: create/open/close, parallel write/broadcast,
  \texttt{H5Pencode}/\texttt{H5Pdecode} round-trip, and \texttt{h5repack}
  cross-file copy for a blob-configured filter (\SectionRef{sec:test-blob}). \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabularx}
\end{center}
